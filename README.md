## Papers



- **[NavWAM: A Navigation World Action Model for Goal-Conditioned Visual Navigation](https://arxiv.org/abs/2606.13494)** — added to *Navigation World models & VLA*. Proposes a diffusion-transformer policy that jointly predicts future observations and goal-progress signals together with actions, enabling tighter closed-loop visual navigation.

- **[Diffusion Transformer World-Action Model for AV Scene Prediction](https://arxiv.org/abs/2606.12987)** — added to *Navigation World models & VLA*. A latent diffusion transformer that predicts future camera frames for autonomous vehicles conditioned on ego-actions, with action-controllable scene generation and uncertainty-calibrated motion forecasting.

- **[Foresight: Iterative Reasoning About Clues that Matter for Navigation](https://arxiv.org/abs/2606.12550)** — added to *Navigation World models & VLA*. A test-time framework in which a VLM alternates between proposing motion plans and self-critiquing them using language goals, boosting navigation task success by 37% without additional training.

- **[PersonaDrive: Human-Style Retrieval-Augmented VLA Agents for Closed-Loop Driving Simulation](https://arxiv.org/abs/2606.12616)** — added to *Navigation World models & VLA*. A VLA driving agent that retrieves matching human demonstration snippets to condition closed-loop behavior on a specified style, enabling diverse behavioral agent populations without per-style retraining.

- **[VLGA: Vision-Language-Geometry-Action Models for Autonomous Driving](https://arxiv.org/abs/2606.12396)** — added to *Navigation World models & VLA*. Extends the VLA paradigm for autonomous driving by adding geometry as a fourth modality—a dedicated expert head supervised by per-pixel pointmap regression against LiDAR—achieving state-of-the-art open-loop results on nuScenes and Bench2Drive without ego status input.

- **[World Engine: Towards the Era of Post-Training for Autonomous Driving](https://arxiv.org/abs/2606.19836)** — added to *Navigation World models & VLA*. Generative framework that reconstructs high-fidelity interactive driving environments and systematically extrapolates safety-critical scenario variations to enable reinforcement-based post-training of end-to-end driving policies.

- **[HEAT: Heterogeneous End-to-End Autonomous Driving via Trajectory-Guided World Models](https://arxiv.org/abs/2605.19631)** — added to *Navigation World models & VLA*. Organises end-to-end driving training around planning trajectories and incorporates a world model predicting future latent features, achieving substantial improvements on nuScenes, NAVSIM, and Waymo benchmarks.
